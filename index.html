1st Time Runners is a Bengaluru-based running club dedicated to helping beginners lace up and take their first step. Founded by ultra runner Karthick AR, we provide structured training, community support, and race preparation — all with zero judgment.

We run at Cubbon Park, Kadugudi Tree Park, and at major events across the city. In just 6 months, we've trained over 100 runners. Our year-end prize rewards the most consistent runners.

About Our Founder
KARTHICK AR
Ultra Runner · Coach · Founder

Karthick is an Ultra Runner with over 8 years of endurance running experience. His passion lies in mentoring aspiring runners and building a healthy, active community.

Running Basics
FAQ
What is 1st Time Runners? +
1st Time Runners is a running club helping beginners start their running journey in life, founded in Bengaluru by ultra runner Karthick AR.
Who can join the club? +
Anyone interested in starting to run, regardless of fitness level, age, or background. All are welcome — complete beginners to returning runners.
How many people do you train? +
Our mission is to train 500 individuals per year. In our first 6 months alone, we trained 100+ runners and helped 125+ people start their journey.
What is the training schedule? +
Training sessions are held weekly. We have morning sessions at Cubbon Park and Kadugudi Tree Park. Check our Events page for specific dates.
Do I need special gear? +
Basic running shoes and comfortable clothing are recommended. No fancy equipment needed — just the will to start!
What is the year-end prize? +
We track KMs run by each member throughout the year. At year-end, top performers are recognised and awarded prizes. Track your KMs on the Runners page!
